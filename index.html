Three questions, answered

What is the longest train ride?

The Trans-Siberian Express:
Distance: 5,772 miles / 9,350 km
Duration: +6 days
This legendary train ride crosses 7 different time zones and goes through incredibly diverse landscapes. It was built by Tsar Alexander III, who had the idea to make Russia more accessible. The train departs every 2 days.

What is the state bird of Pennsylvania?

The ruffed grouse is the state bird of Pennsylvania. Scientific name (Bonasa umbellus), this grouse is a chicken-like bird found in the northernmost parts of North America, including Pennsylvania, which is the only state to choose it as its official bird. The ruffed grouse has many unique features: a crest, an overall fan shape, a black-banded tail, and black ruffles on the sides of its neck.

What is the tallest tree in the world in 2021?

The world's largest tree is the Hyperion, which is a coastal redwood (Sequoia sempervirens) and is located somewhere in the heart of Redwood National Park in California.
How tall is the tree? The Hyperion reaches a staggering 380 feet!
